Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans), finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped spring onions for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok. Add garlic and onions, and saut√© until fragrant.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry until they are cooked but still crisp.
  3. If using eggs, push the vegetables to one side of the pan and scramble the eggs in the other side. Once cooked, mix them with the vegetables.
  4. Add the cold rice, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine everything and heat through.
  5. Garnish with chopped spring onions if desired and serve hot.
